A Recipe (really bad bread)

  • flour 3 cups
  • warm water 1 cup
  • sugar 1 tsp
  • yeast 1/4 tsp
  1. add four and sugar to a bowl and make a well in the center
  2. put yeast into the well and add water
  3. mix ingredients until you can knead them, then kenad for 8 minutes
  4. let rise in a warm place for some time (30 mins?)
  5. knead again then bake at 425 for 12 minutes
